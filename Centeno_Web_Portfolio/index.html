4th Year - Capstone

GreenStairs: An IoT Enabled Kinetic Energy Harnessing System With Sustainability Support Application

Green Stairs is a kinetic energy harvesting tile overlay that converts footsteps into usable electrical energy using piezoelectric technology. Designed for staircases in high-traffic areas, it captures kinetic energy from movement and transforms it into power for lighting or low-power devices. A connected mobile and web app monitors energy output, system status, and usage analytics in real time. Green Stairs supports smart infrastructure and promotes sustainable energy solutions in everyday environments.
